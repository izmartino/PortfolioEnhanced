GET TO KNOW ME

First of all: I LOVE COFFEE

I started drinking coffee when I was a teenager. I used to observe my parents brew a cup of coffee to start the day, and I was always fascinated by the aroma. Coffee is more than simply a beverage to me; it's a ritual. It's an opportunity to pause and appreciate the flavor and aroma throughout a hectic day.

In conclusion, I consider coffee to be more than just a beverage. It's a way of life and a passion. I adore how it tastes, how it makes me feel, and the happy and supportive moments it brings into my life.

But I'm acidic

Despite being acidic, my affection for coffee never faded. I was determined to discover a painless way to consume my favorite beverage. By trial and error, I learned that some varieties of coffee, such dark roasts and cold brews, were less acidic and easier on my stomach. In order to balance the acid, I also discovered that drinking coffee with a little meal or snack is a good idea.
